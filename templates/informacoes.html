Fundus images classification

This project has the objective to analyze fundus images and classify them in two types: Positive or negative for Diabetic Retinopathy.

Growing number of carriers

Diabetes affects about half a million people worldwide. The perspective is that by 2045 about 700 million people will have this condition.

Primary cause of blindness in people aged between 20 and 74 years.

According to the Brazilian Diabetes Society, Diabetic Retinopathy is the leading cause of blindness in people aged between 20 and 74 years, in addition to being the most common diabetes-related pathology.

Presence of Retinopathy

There are about 2 million people in Brazil with some degree of Retinopathy and it's growth is related to the growing number of diabetics.
